About NodPod

Our Mission

At NodPod, we're dedicated to revolutionizing tactical gear through innovative design and uncompromising quality. Our mission is to provide operators with the tools they need to perform at their peak, no matter the conditions.

Meet the Team

Tom

Founder & CEO

After a decade as a Paratrooper in the US Army, where he served on a team in a highly specialized Long-Range Reconnaissance and Surveillance (LRRS) unit, Tom took to the private sector, pursuing executive protection, investigations, and surveillance. There, he applied his passion for engineering and creative solutions, landing him in the world of CAD, 3D printing, and laser CNC technology.

His work is characterized by precision, creativity, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Beyond his technical prowess, Tom holds several licenses, including FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot, FCC Radio Technician, Private Investigator, PPO, and many more, which provides valuable context behind our products.

Tori

Head of Product Development

Tori is very detail-oriented with experience in the production industry as a final script supervisor and payroll manager, making her skilled with numbers, details, and delegation. She has extensive experience in commercial childcare, where she applied her degree in psychology, providing her with a deep understanding of human behavior and the importance of empathy in leadership.

In addition to her professional achievements, Tori is experienced in English and Western horseback competition riding, a practice demanding discipline, finesse, and a harmonious connection between horse and rider. This experience has taught her the value of patience, practice, and the pursuit of excellence. Her vision for the company is to expand into new industries and build a larger, stronger company on the foundation of duty, responsibility, and treating others with kindness.

Why NodPod?

We believe that the right gear can make all the difference. Our products are born from real-world experience and rigorous testing, ensuring that when it matters most, your equipment won't let you down.
